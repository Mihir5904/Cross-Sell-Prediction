About This Insurance Prediction Model

Input Features Explained:

  • Age: User's age group.
  • Vehicle Age: Age of the user's vehicle.
  • Annual Premium: Insurance premium amount.
  • Gender: Male or Female.
  • Previously Insured: If the customer had insurance before.
  • Vehicle Damage: Whether the vehicle was previously damaged.
  • Vintage: Customer relationship in days (converted to months).
